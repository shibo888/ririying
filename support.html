Moola Support

Moola is a local profit calculator for business owners. It helps estimate daily revenue, costs, break-even points, and profit directly on your device.

Contact

If you need help with Moola, please email us at shenshibo7@gmail.com.

Please include your device model, iOS version, and a short description of the issue so we can respond more efficiently.

Using Moola

  • Enter your revenue and cost figures in the app.
  • Tap the confirmation button to calculate and review the results.
  • All calculations are performed locally on your device.
  • Moola does not require account registration or login.

Troubleshooting

  • Make sure your numbers are entered in the correct fields.
  • If results look unexpected, review your cost settings and operating days.
  • If the app becomes unresponsive, close it and reopen it.
  • If the problem continues, contact us by email with details.

Privacy

Moola does not collect personal data, does not upload your inputs, does not show ads, and does not use third-party analytics SDKs.

For details, please read the Privacy Policy.
