Privacy Policy

Moola Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how Moola handles data. Moola is designed as a local profit calculation tool and does not collect personal information.

Summary

  • Moola does not require login or account registration.
  • Moola does not collect personal data.
  • Moola does not upload revenue, cost, or profit inputs to any server.
  • Moola does not include advertising.
  • Moola does not use third-party analytics SDKs.
  • All calculations are performed locally on your device.

Information We Collect

We do not collect personal information through Moola. The app is built to work without user registration, user accounts, or cloud storage.

Business figures you enter, such as revenue, costs, margins, operating days, and profit targets, are processed locally on your device and are not uploaded by us.

Local Processing

Moola performs calculations on your device. Any values you enter are used only to generate calculation results inside the app.

If the app stores settings or recent calculation information, that information remains on your device unless you choose to remove the app or clear related device data.

Third Parties

Moola does not integrate advertising networks or third-party analytics SDKs.

If Moola offers optional in-app purchases, payment and purchase processing are handled by Apple through the App Store. We do not receive your payment card information.

Children's Privacy

Moola is intended for business calculation use and is not directed to children. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy when needed. Updates will be posted on this page with a new updated date.
